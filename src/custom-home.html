MODULAR LEARNING OFFER

Step by step to a smaller footprint

The Climate Academy supports your company in tackling the climate crisis and meeting your climate protection and sustainability goals. The modular learning offer provides your employees with the necessary awareness, knowledge and crucial skills to identify emission sources in the company, identify ESG hurdles and develop sustainable strategies and solutions.

  • Essentials: Raising awareness and basic knowledge for the entire workforce in order to understand the causes and effects of climate change as well as measures to mitigate it and to be able to take active action.

  • Deep Dives: Departmental, topic- and industry-specific learning paths in which in-depth ESG content for the individual challenges of everyday work is highlighted, good practices are presented and employees are encouraged and trained to develop solution strategies.

PERSONALIZED CONTENT

Turn your company into your own module

ESG and sustainability challenges can vary greatly from company to company. That's why, on request, we also offer the opportunity to expand our existing modular learning offer with further personalized and company-specific modules in order to address individual challenges and goals and convey them quickly and effectively to your workforce.
