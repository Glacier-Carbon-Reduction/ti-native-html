What is the Download Hub?

 

It's your one-stop shop for all the valuable resources from our sustainability modules. No more navigating back and forth through different chapters to find what you need. 📂

Here's how you can dive in:

  • Look for the Download symbol (a downward arrow 📥) in the top bar of your platform.
  • Click on it, and voilà! You'll see all the modules you've explored.
  • Choose any module and download its documents – factsheets, summaries, extra reading materials, and more.
  • You can download each document separately or grab them all in one go with a handy zip file. Convenience at its best!
