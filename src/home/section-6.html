Acting with brains

In order to cope with the climate transformation, application-oriented climate protection competencies are needed in order to be able to implement measures quickly, specifically and directly within one's own company. As part of the Glacier Climate Academy, we therefore not only provide your employees with the necessary awareness, but also enable them to implement it immediately in their everyday work. Through concrete recommendations for action, good practices as well as practical examples from various industries and interactive elements to consolidate the newly learned skills.
