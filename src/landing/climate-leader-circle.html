Empowering and Connecting Climate Leaders along their sustainability Journey

Module 1

Sustainability Department 2.0

• What is the new role of Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) and how is it evolving in today's business landscape?

• What are the structural and regulatory requirements that companies must adhere to in order to effectively implement CSR initiatives?

• What are the necessary competencies and skills that individuals and organisations must possess in order to successfully navigate the complex and evolving world of CSR?

Module 2

Strategic Climate Action

• How do a company's corporate objectives align with its climate goals, and how can these goals be effectively integrated into overall business strategy?

• What are the best practices for engaging stakeholders, such as employees, investors, and customers, in the process of setting and achieving climate goals?

• How can companies communicate their climate goals effectively to stakeholders, and what are the key factors to consider in crafting an impactful communication strategy?

Module 3

How to Build a Green Team

• How can companies effectively engage and motivate their teams to participate in the green transformation of their organisation, and what are the benefits of doing so?

• What are the best practices for collaborating with other companies and stakeholders in order to achieve shared sustainability goals and promote systemic change?

• What are the key factors to consider when undergoing an internal green transformation, and how can companies effectively implement sustainable practices and behaviours throughout their organisation?

Module 4

Green Skills for a sustainable business

• What are the key skills and competencies needed in a team to effectively drive sustainability initiatives, and how can companies develop and nurture these skills?

• How can companies integrate sustainability into their HR and overall business strategy, and what are the benefits of doing so?

• How can companies leverage sustainability as a key aspect of their employer branding and use it to attract, retain, and engage employees who are passionate about making a positive impact?
