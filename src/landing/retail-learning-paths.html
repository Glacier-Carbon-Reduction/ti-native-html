Climate Change 101

Module 2

Introduction to ESG

• The importance of ESG for companies and the relationship between environmental, social and governance factors together

• The differences between carbon neutral, net zero, CO2 positive

• The role of companies, individuals, and consumers*

Module 3

ESG in Retail

• Key ESG issues for retailers: (circular economy, energy, locations)

• Regulatory and market developments and ESG regulations

Module 4

Module 5

Sustainable Supply Chains

• Impact of production and supply processes on the environment

• Sustainable supply chain design from procurement to production and use to disposal
